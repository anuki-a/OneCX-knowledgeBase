# Kiosk Script

**Intro — Overview**  
Welcome to OneCX\! In this video, we’ll walk through the Kiosk Application — the customer-facing interface that enables check-ins, appointments, and walk-ins at your service locations. You’ll see how the kiosk reflects the settings configured in the admin portal and how customers interact with it in real time.  
\[Show kiosk home screen with sign-in/check-in/schedule cards.\]

**Home Page**  
This is the home page. The visible cards—Sign In, Appointment Check-In, and Schedule a future Appointment—are determined by the configuration we saw earlier.

**Sign-In Flow**  
Clicking Sign In, the visitor is prompted to select products or services. If the ‘Show Categories’ option was enabled, services are grouped by category.

They can select one or more and view their choices as chips at the top. Once done, clicking ‘Next’ leads to a ‘Sign In Details’ entry form.

**Entering Sign In Details**  
Here, they can enter personal details such as first name, last name, and optional details such as account number, phone number, email address, date of birth, or even custom notes. 

Visitors can opt in for text messages as well if a phone number is specified. If Staff Preference or Group Selection is enabled, they can choose accordingly. Then, they complete sign-in.

**Confirmation Screen**  
Post sign-in, a confirmation page displays visitor position, estimated wait time, and check in / appointment time—exactly as configured. The user will be automatically directed back to the ‘Home’ page after the configured time in the related kiosk settings.

**Check-In for Appointment**  
Moving back to the ‘Home’ page in the Check-In section, visitors can validate an existing appointment by entering the appointment confirmation number or last name and appointment time upon arriving at the branch's location. If details don’t match, an error is shown. If correct, they are successfully checked in.

**Schedule an Appointment**  
Choosing Schedule a Future Appointment launches the Appointments Web Widget, allowing customers to book future appointments right from the kiosk.

And that’s a look at how the Kiosk Application works in OneCX. From intuitive navigation to real-time updates, the kiosk ensures a smooth and personalized experience for every customer.
