# Home Script

## **Opening Scene – Page loads with the dashboard visible**

Welcome to OneCX – your centralized dashboard for managing customer interactions efficiently. In this video, we’ll walk through the ‘Home’ page which details a summarized view of Appointments and Lobby Check-In details associated with the logged in user.

## **Scene shifts to the top bar – location dropdown and summary bar**

The Today's Service Summary panel below gives you a quick glance at key metrics—like how many visitors are currently waiting, the average wait time, the number of staff signed in, and total visitors served today.  
Below the summary stats bar, you’ll find the current location settings. This determines which branch’s data is shown. By default, the first accessible location—based on the alphabetical order of the logged-in user’s assigned location will be displayed.

## **Scene scrolls to 'My Check-In Requests'**

Next, the 'My Check-In Requests' section shows all customer check-ins assigned to the logged in staff member for today. These check-ins may come through directly assisted staff entries in the Lobby or self-service Kiosks. You'll see the time, request details, location, and how long each visitor has been waiting.

## **Scene scrolls to 'My Appointment Requests'**

Below that, is the 'My Appointment Requests' panel. Here, you can view all appointments scheduled with the logged in staff member—either for today, tomorrow, or this week. Each row shows the time, request details, location, and the expected duration of the appointment.

## **Scene pans to the two graphs at the bottom**

At the bottom of the Home page, you'll find two useful visual summaries. The first graph shows appointments booked by location, helping you see where activities concentrated across your accessible branches. The second shows visitors currently waiting by location, so you can stay aware of customer traffic across the accessible locations.

## **Closing Scene – zooms out to show the whole page briefly**

This Home dashboard is designed to give you real-time visibility into your day—empowering you to deliver smoother, faster service. And remember, all data shown here is tailored to your access level and role in the system.

For more tutorials, check out the rest of our video series or reach out to your onboarding specialist.
