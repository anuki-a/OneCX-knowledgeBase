# Kiosk Configuration Script

**Intro — Overview**  
Welcome to the OneCX platform — your all-in-one solution for managing customer experiences across locations. In this video, we’ll explore the Kiosk Configuration module — where you can define how each kiosk behaves for specific branches, customize settings, and control the overall flow of your customer check-ins.  
\[Show homepage and navigate to Configuration \> Kiosks.\]

**Accessing Kiosk Configuration**  
To begin, go to the left navigation panel, click on the ‘Configuration’ section, and select the ‘Kiosks’ option.

**Kiosk Configuration**  
Here you’ll see a list of all available active branches under the client. Branches that already have kiosks configured will display action buttons, such as Edit, Delete, and Launch. Branches without configuration will show a plus icon to add a new kiosk setup.

**Editing Kiosk Configuration Settings**  
Whether you're configuring a new kiosk setup for the first time via the “plus” icon or editing the configuration setup of an existing one through the ‘edit’ icon, the layout is the same.  Let’s look at the kiosk settings for a configured branch. The settings are organized into functional categories:

**Check-in Display Options**  
First, we have Check-in Display Options. These control the visibility of sign-in, check-in, and appointment scheduling cards in Kiosk.

**Optional Input Fields**  
Next, we have up to four optional fields—like Account Number, Phone Number, Email Address, Date of Birth or Custom. You can mark these as visible, mandatory, or both.

**Staff Preference Options**  
Enable Staff Preference Options if you want customers to select a staff member or group during check-in. A group can be set to display by default as well.

---

**Product/Service Options**  
Under Product/Service Options, control the visibility, sorting order, font size, and whether to show product categories. You can also make service selection mandatory.

---

**Wait Time & Queue Display Options**  
This section lets you show real-time wait times and queue positions both on the home page and on the confirmation screen. Choose how to calculate this wait time—based on average over time, visitors served, or real-time queue conditions.

**Additional Settings**  
In Additional Settings, configure welcome and thank-you messages, refresh intervals, default group notifications, and language preferences including Spanish speaker support.

**Saving the configurations** – Click on “Save” to update changes made to the ‘Kiosk Configuration’ page. 

**Cancelling changes** – Click on “Cancel” to discard the changes made to the ‘Kiosk Configuration’ page.

**Adding a new Kiosk**   
To configure a new kiosk, click the ‘plus’ icon. A default template will appear, which you can adjust and then save.

**Copy Configuration**  
Alternatively, use the Copy Settings option to duplicate settings from an existing kiosk to a new branch.

**Launch or Delete Kiosk**  
You can also launch a kiosk for testing or deleting its configuration using the available action icons.

---

And that’s a walkthrough of the ’Kiosk Configuration’ module in OneCX.  
With these settings, you have full control over how your kiosks function — from what customers see to how their journey is managed.
