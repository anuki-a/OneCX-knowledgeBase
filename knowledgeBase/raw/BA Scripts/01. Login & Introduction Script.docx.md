# Getting Started & Navigation

Welcome to OneCX — a powerful tool designed to streamline visitor management for your bank or credit union. Let’s start by logging in as a Client Admin. 

Enter your username and password, then click Login.

# **After Login – Client Recognition**

Once logged in, OneCX will automatically identify your financial institution, and you'll see your Client Name prominently displayed in the header.

# **Sign-In Popup – Branch Selection**

Next, a Sign-In popup appears, showing all the active branches accessible by the logged in user’s account.   
Select the branch you'd like to manage and click “Sign In to Lobby Queue” to proceed.

# **Top Header Panel Overview**

Now let’s explore the top header panel. Here, you’ll see your client’s name, helping you confirm you're managing the correct account.

* Click the bell icon to view OneCX system alerts, including updates and important notifications.  
* Click your username in the top-right corner to access:  
  * My Account,  
  * My Alerts, and  
  * Log Out option  
* You can also switch signed-in branches or sign out of your current branch using the Sign In/Out icon.

# **Left Menu Panel Overview – High-Level Navigation**

The left menu panel gives you access to all major areas of the OneCX application. Here's a quick overview:

* **Home** – View a summary of today's lobby check-in and appointment activities.  
    
* **Lobby** – Manage real-time visitor traffic through lobby and appointment queues and view signed in/out staff status for today across accessible branches.  
    
* **Appointments** – Manage appointments, exceptions and events, view action center, appointment reports and appointments administration related settings.  
    
* **User Management** section:  
* **Users** – Add and manage user profiles within your client organization.  
* **Permission Groups** – Add and manage notification groups to handle alert notifications and permission groups to define permissions and access levels.


* **Locations & Services** section:   
* **Locations** \- Add and manage organization, area, region and branch locations within your client organization.  
* **Product Categories** – Organize your products/services under customized categories.  
* **Products & Services** – Configure specific product/service offerings available for appointments or walk-ins.  
* **Checklists** – Define checklists customized for each product/service to support consistent service delivery.


* **Configuration** section:   
* **Global Settings** – Manage system-wide settings and controls.  
* **Kiosks** – Set up and manage check-in kiosks deployed at your branches.  
* **Text Messages & Global Alerts** – Configure automated messaging and alerts to enhance communication.  
* **Left Without Assistance** – Configure reasons for visitors leaving the branch without assistance.  
* **Data Import/Export** – Import configuration data or export usage data files as needed.  
* **Localization** – Adjust labels and language settings to suit your environment.


* **Reports** section:   
* **Lobby Activity –** Access to real–time and historical lobby activity reports.  
* **Lobby Editor** – Track and edit lobby-related report information.
