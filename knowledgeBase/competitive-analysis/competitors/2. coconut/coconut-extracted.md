# Coconut Software - Product Analysis

## 1. Product Purpose

- **Intelligent Branch Solutions platform** purpose-built for banks and credit unions to optimize branch operations and customer engagement [CR1]
- Unifies appointment scheduling, lobby management (queue/visitor management), video banking, workforce management, and analytics in one connected platform [CR1][CR2]
- Enables financial institutions to capture and route customer demand across every channel (appointments, walk-ins, digital) [CR1]
- Transforms branch operations from reactive to predictive by using AI-powered forecasting and intelligent matching [CR3][CR4]
- Designed to drive better outcomes from every advisor interaction by connecting clients with the right expert at the right moment [CR1]

## 2. Target Customers

- **Primary:** Banks and credit unions across North America [CR1][CR2]
- **Size:** Trusted by 200+ financial institutions including enterprise organizations [CR3]
- **Named customers:** RBC Royal Bank, Mountain America Credit Union (MACU), M&T Bank, Arvest Bank, Vancity, Rogue Credit Union, UMassFive College Federal Credit Union, Bank of New Hampshire, Teachers Federal Credit Union, Kawartha Credit Union, CU SoCal, National Bank, Centier Bank, UFCU, Partners Federal Credit Union, UNIFY Financial Credit Union [CR1][CR2][CR14 - Case Study]
- Organizations seeking to improve loan pull-through rates, reduce handle time, and raise customer satisfaction [CR2]
- Financial institutions with complex branch operations, multiple lines of business, and high-value client journeys [CR1]

## 3. Business Capabilities

- **Appointment Scheduling:** Self-serve online booking with real-time calendar sync across channels [CR2][CR5]
- **Lobby Management:** Digital queue management for walk-in traffic with wait time tracking and virtual queuing [CR2][CR6]
- **Video Banking (Coconut Connect):** Browser-based secure video meetings with identity verification, e-signature, screen sharing, and co-browsing [CR7]
- **Branch Workforce Management (bWFM):** AI-powered demand forecasting, staff planning, and shift management [CR3][CR8]
- **Branch Insights & Analytics:** Cross-channel reporting, performance benchmarking, conversational analytics, and automated alerts [CR9]
- **Intelligent Matching (Meet on Demand):** Routes clients to the right advisor based on expertise, language, relationship value, and availability [CR1][CR10]
- **Contact Center Support:** Unified scheduling and queue management for remote teams [CR14 - Case Study]
- **Reserve with Google:** Direct booking from Google Search, Maps, and Assistant [CR5]

## 5. User Workflows

### Client Booking Workflow [CR2][CR5]
1. Client accesses booking via website, Google Search/Maps, or digital banking platform
2. Selects location, service type, and preferred date/time
3. System shows available advisors based on service and expertise
4. Client completes custom questions/forms
5. Receives confirmation and automated reminders
6. Joins appointment (in-branch, video, or phone)

### Walk-in Management Workflow [CR6][CR11 - Help Center]
1. Client arrives at branch or joins virtual queue
2. Checks in via kiosk, staff tablet, or mobile device
3. Answers custom intake questions
4. Receives wait time estimate
5. Gets next-in-line notification via SMS/email
6. Staff facilitates appointment or converts to scheduled appointment

### Staff Appointment Workflow [CR14 - Case Study][CR11 - Help Center]
1. Staff views daily schedule with appointment details
2. Reviews client information and meeting purpose in advance
3. Receives notifications for upcoming appointments
4. Conducts meeting (in-branch or video)
5. Takes notes and creates follow-up tasks within system
6. Logs outcomes for reporting

### Workforce Planning Workflow [CR8]
1. System analyzes historical appointment, walk-in, and transaction data
2. AI generates demand forecasts by branch, time, and service type
3. Manager reviews forecasts and skill requirements
4. System generates recommended schedules
5. Manager adjusts for absences and rebalances coverage
6. Staff receives schedules and updates availability

### Video Banking Workflow [CR7]
1. Appointment scheduled with video option
2. System generates unique secure join link
3. Client receives link via email/SMS
4. Both parties join browser-based meeting (no downloads)
5. Staff shares screen, collects e-signatures, captures ID
6. System records audio and generates transcript
7. Staff completes notes and follow-up tasks

## 7. AI Capabilities

### AI-Powered Demand Forecasting [CR3][CR8]
- Transforms historical appointments, walk-ins, and transaction data into branch-level forecasts
- Predicts high-traffic periods and coverage needs
- Generates daily, weekly, and monthly demand projections
- Enables proactive staffing decisions with 16% greater accuracy

### Intelligent Matching [CR1][CR10]
- Uses intent, skills, availability, and business context to route clients
- Matches based on expertise, language, and relationship value
- Reduces rebookings and increases conversion
- Optimizes "best available" vs. "next available" advisor selection

### Smart Scheduling [CR8]
- Converts demand forecasts into complete staff-ready schedules in minutes
- Recommends time-of-day headcount and skill mix
- Reclaims up to 8 hours per week of manual scheduling time for branch managers

### Conversational Analytics (Beta) [CR18 - Release Notes][CR9]
- Plain-language query interface for data exploration
- Ask questions like "Which branches had the highest no-show rate last quarter?"
- Returns charts, tables, or summaries powered by trusted analytics layer
- Available to Advanced Analytics Edit Users (launched June 2026)

### Human-Centered AI Philosophy [CR1]
- AI optimizes matching, forecasting, and performance
- Goal is to enable more effective human interactions, not replace them
- Focus on augmenting advisor capabilities

### Weaknesses

**Industry-Specific Focus**
- Purpose-built for financial institutions may limit applicability to other industries
- Not positioned as general-purpose visitor/customer engagement platform

**No Information Found On:**
- Pricing transparency (not publicly disclosed)
- Mobile app availability for staff or clients
- Offline capabilities
- Multi-language support specifics beyond "Languages in Coconut: Overview"
- Maximum scale limits (concurrent users, locations, appointments)
- Disaster recovery and uptime SLAs
- Third-party marketplace or app ecosystem

**Integration Limitations** [CR16 - Integrations]

**Complexity for Smaller Institutions**
- Feature-rich platform may be overwhelming for small credit unions or single-branch banks
- "Extensive configurability" suggests learning curve
