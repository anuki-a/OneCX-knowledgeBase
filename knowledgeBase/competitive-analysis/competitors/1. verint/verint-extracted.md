## 5. User Workflows

### Branch Customer Engagement Workflow [VR3]
- Customer schedules appointment through booking system
- System optimizes branch staffing based on appointment volume
- Queue management directs customer flow upon arrival
- Branch staff serve customers based on optimized schedules
- System tracks service metrics and customer satisfaction

### Experience Management Workflow [VR4]
- System deploys surveys across 341+ branch locations
- Collects 200,000+ annual customer responses
- Aggregates feedback into centralized dashboard
- Analyzes NPS scores and satisfaction metrics
- Identifies improvement opportunities
- Governance framework implements changes

### Quality Assurance Workflow [VR8]
- Quality Bot automatically evaluates customer interactions
- AI analyzes 14x more interactions than manual review
- Identifies compliance issues and quality gaps
- Flags interactions for supervisor review
- Provides agent performance feedback
- Tracks quality score improvements over time

### Coaching & Sales Workflow [VR7]
- Coaching Bot monitors agent interactions in real-time
- Identifies up-sell and cross-sell opportunities
- Provides real-time guidance to agents
- Tracks offer acceptance rates
- Measures sales conversion improvements
- Delivers targeted coaching based on performance

### Compliance Recording Workflow [VR6]
- System captures regulated interactions across all channels
- Applies pause-and-resume for sensitive data protection
- Stores recordings with retention policies
- AI transcribes and indexes content
- Surfaces key moments for compliance review
- Enables search and retrieval for audits
