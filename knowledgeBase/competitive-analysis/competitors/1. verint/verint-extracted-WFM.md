# Verint Workforce Management - Competitive Analysis Extract

## 1. Product Purpose

- AI-powered workforce management software to meet service and CX goals at lower cost with accurate forecasting, flexible scheduling, and real-time data insights [VWM1]
- Predicts work volumes and customer demand for future time periods based on historical and present data, trends, and external factors, then aligns employee schedules to cost-effectively meet that demand [VWM2]
- Simplifies and automates the complex task of forecasting and scheduling, even with large numbers of employees, complex scheduling periods, and many queues to staffing profiles [VWM3]
- Helps organizations lower costs by staffing appropriately to meet workload, drive business growth and operational excellence, and improve employee engagement and retention [VWM3]
- Enables contact centers, back office, and branch operations to have the right people with the right skills at the right time [VWM1, VWM2]
- Eliminates overstaffing and unnecessary overtime while reducing attrition and increasing employee engagement [VWM2]

## 5. User Workflows

### Forecast Creation Workflow [VWM2]
- System analyzes historical volumes, work types, and handle times across all channels
- AI incorporates external events and data that impact volumes
- WFM analyst builds custom forecasting logic with interval-level control
- System generates forecast with 95%+ accuracy
- Analyst conducts what-if scenarios for decision making
- Real-time adjustments made with percentage-based overrides
- Forecasts automatically update without rerunning schedules

### Schedule Generation Workflow [VWM3, VWM5]
- Auto-scheduler analyzes demand forecast, employee preferences, and labor laws
- System generates optimized schedules in few clicks
- Planner within WFM team optimizes schedules
- Schedules passed to managers one week in advance
- Managers monitor adherence and attendance
- Team leaders instructed to scale staffing up or down
- Employees access schedules via mobile app 24/7

### TimeFlex Schedule Change Workflow [VWM7]
- Agent needs schedule change (e.g., doctor appointment)
- Agent accesses TimeFlex Bot via mobile or desktop
- System shows FlexCoin balance and interval values
- Agent selects intervals to change (e.g., modify start time from 9am to noon)
- System calculates FlexCoin impact in real-time
- If agent has positive balance, change approved automatically without manager intervention
- Agent picks up hours on another day to maintain total hours
- System ensures overall schedule quality maintained or improved
- No manager approval needed; change takes effect immediately

### Shift Swap Workflow [VWM3]
- Employee posts shift swap request via mobile app
- Information forwarded to managers for processing
- Other employees view available swaps
- Employee negotiates and requests swap
- Manager receives notification for quick approval
- System updates schedules automatically

### Intraday Management Workflow [VWM2, VWM3]
- System monitors real-time contact volume, handle time, service levels
- Deviations from forecast trigger email alerts and screen pop-ups
- Manager views real-time dashboard with projections
- Manager adjusts staffing: schedules overtime or offers voluntary time off
- System automatically modifies schedules based on changes
- Employees notified of changes via mobile app
- Adherence tracking continues in real-time

### Employee Availability Management Workflow [VWM5]
- Employee indicates availability preferences in Flex Manager
- WFM team reviews availability across time zones and languages
- Scheduler considers employee preferences (e.g., avoiding night shifts)
- Auto-scheduler generates schedules respecting availability
- Employee receives schedule one week in advance
- Employee can make further adjustments via TimeFlex Bot

### Time Registration and Payroll Workflow [VWM5]
- Employee logs in via MyTime module
- Time registration captured automatically
- Data shared directly with payroll system
- System accommodates in-country labor laws and regulations
- Payroll processed with accurate time data
- Supports scalability across multiple countries

## 12. Interesting Ideas

- **FlexCoin Economy**: Innovative gamification approach where agents earn virtual currency for business-beneficial schedule changes and spend it for personal flexibility, creating win-win scenario [VWM7]
- **Unlimited Schedule Changes Without Manager Intervention**: TimeFlex Bot's proprietary model ensures sum of all agent-initiated changes maintains or improves schedule quality, fundamentally changing manager workload [VWM7]
- **Split Shift Flexibility**: Agents can split shifts (e.g., work 3 hours in morning, 3 hours in evening) to accommodate personal needs while meeting business requirements [VWM7]
- **15-Minute Interval FlexCoin Valuation**: Every 15-minute interval dynamically assigned value based on real-time forecast, capacity, and performance, creating precise supply-demand marketplace [VWM7]
- **Real-Time Model Recalculation**: As employees make changes, the TimeFlex model stays current, ensuring continuous optimization [VWM7]
- **Dialogue-Based Change Management**: 5CA's approach of collaborative engagement with employees ("Why is 'x' important? What are the pain points?") to drive adoption and engagement [VWM5]
- **Open Data Architecture**: Verint's approach allows customers to use their BI tools of choice (e.g., Power BI) for enhanced reporting rather than forcing proprietary reporting [VWM5]
- **Two-Day Rapid Scaling**: Ability to onboard 90+ employees in 5 languages in just 2 days demonstrates unprecedented agility [VWM5]
- **Work-From-Home Optimization**: Purpose-built capabilities for distributed, remote workforces with time zone and language management [VWM5]
- **Interval-Level Forecast Control**: Granular control over workload, SLA, shrinkage, occupancy, and efficiency at interval level, not just daily or hourly [VWM2]
- **What-If Scenario Modeling**: AI-powered scenario planning enables faster, data-driven decision making before committing to changes [VWM2]
- **Percentage-Based Real-Time Overrides**: Adjust forecasts in real-time with controlled overrides without rerunning entire schedules [VWM2]
- **Voluntary Time Off Projections**: System proactively identifies overstaffing periods and enables employees to request VTO that aligns with their skills and proficiencies [VWM3]
- **Mobile-First Employee Experience**: Comprehensive mobile app for all employee self-service needs, not just schedule viewing [VWM3]
