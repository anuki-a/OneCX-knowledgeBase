# Competitive Capability Analysis

## Capability Groups

1. **Appointment Scheduling** - Multi-channel booking, calendar sync, automated reminders, real-time availability, video/phone/in-person appointments, skill-based routing

2. **Queue & Lobby Management** - Virtual queuing, physical queue management, wait time tracking, kiosk check-in, SMS notifications, visitor flow optimization, queue displays

3. **Workforce Management** - AI-powered demand forecasting, staff scheduling, shift management, adherence tracking, employee self-service, capacity planning, intraday management

4. **AI & Automation** - Conversational AI agents, voice agents, chatbots, intelligent matching, auto-summarization, policy-aware workflows, generative AI, transaction automation

5. **Event Management** - Group scheduling, one-to-many booking, RSVP workflows, QR code check-in, guest list management, VIP workflows, event registration

6. **Analytics & Reporting** - Real-time dashboards, interactive reports, conversational analytics, peer benchmarking, KPI tracking, performance metrics, ROI reporting

7. **Knowledge Management** - AI-powered search, content governance, employee self-service, customer self-service, procedure builders, web content ingestion, version control

8. **Integration & APIs** - CRM integrations, calendar sync, contact center platforms, core banking systems, REST APIs, webhooks, pre-built connectors, custom integrations

9. **Video Banking/Conferencing** - Browser-based video, screen sharing, e-signature integration, identity verification, co-browsing, recording and transcription

10. **Mobile & Self-Service** - Mobile apps for staff, mobile apps for customers, employee self-service portals, schedule management, shift swaps, time-off requests

11. **Compliance & Security** - Audit trails, policy guardrails, SOC2/ISO certifications, encryption, role-based access control, PII redaction, regulatory compliance

12. **Customer Experience Features** - Personalization, customer recognition, context preservation, omnichannel continuity, seamless escalation, customer journey orchestration
